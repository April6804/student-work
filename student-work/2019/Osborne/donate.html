Monetary Donations

A gift to the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention helps fund our mission to save lives and give hope to those affected by suicide. All donations to AFSP are tax deductible. If you have questions about your gift or about how to give, please contact our development office at development@afsp.org or by calling toll free, 1-888-333-AFSP. Thank you for your support.
