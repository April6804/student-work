OUR MISSION


United We Dream is the largest immigrant youth-led community in the country. We create welcoming spaces for young people-regardless of immigration status-to support, engage, and empower them to make their voice be heard and win!

We have an online reach of over 4 million and are made up for over 400,00 members are woman and 20% are made up of fearless youth fightting to imrpove the lives of ourselves, our families and our communities. Our vision is a society which our diversity and we believe in leading a multi-ethnic, ointersectional path to get there.

Whether we're organzing in the streets, the building cutting edge technology systems, opening doors for LGBTQ immigrant youth, clearing pathways to education, stopping deportations or creating alliances across social movements, United We Dream puts undocumented immigrant youth in the driver's seat to strategize, innovate and win.
