CONTACT US FOR MORE INFO!

You can easily reach us by email at info@louisvillecream.com We can send you our current flavor list to start getting ideas, and help you with designing a custom flavor! You'll also get a list of needs from us (information on the event, parking, payment, etc), and what our catering services provide for you.

And remember, we love all of you - even those with dietary restrictions! If you can't, or don't want, diary, nuts, gluten, soy, etc. we can make a custom option for you such as:

  • Gluten free
  • Vegan ice cream or sorbet
  • Soy free

We believe in tweaking an existing flavor to work around an allergy or restriction When it comes to dessert, we believe no one should be left out - seriously.
