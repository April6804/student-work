What is The Trevor Project?

The Trevor Project is a life changing movement with the mission to help LGBTQ+ youth. With a life saving hotline for youth in crisis, to helping others learn about suicide prevention; The Trevor project is on a mission to save young lives in need.

The Trevor Project was founded in 1998 and grew faster than anyone could account for. Young people from all over began reaching out looking for help and we answered the call. Sixteen years later The Trevor Project has grown to become the leading force in LGBTQ+ suicide prevention. After such growth the project is now moving forward and expanding it's horizons.

Now with the help of millions of donators the project can now offer new resources to everyone. The Trevor Project is now offering classes to help prevent suicide prevention, along with providing better more efficent help for any minor in need.
