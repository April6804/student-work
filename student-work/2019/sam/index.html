Welcome To All

The Museum of Modern Art is a place for everyone from different cultures; to come together and share their views or resonate to the art. Every week; we present different art, and then we express open mindedness about the art.

Discover the World of Art

The Museum of Modern Art displays the work of artists with a personal description of materials used to create the art; along with their personal thoughts during the process. The ideas and issues are expressed in many forms of art such as the following Painting, sculpture, glass making, woodwork, wired art, pen and ink, abstract, and sketching displays. provides insight into how certain creative endeavors push traditional process beyond the limits of conventional practice and allow materials to inform and resonate personal, historical, social, and political issues.
