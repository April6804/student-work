About Us

We love visitors!

We love having visitors of all ages! We are open to the public Mon-Thurs 9am-4pm free of charge! We have guided tours Mon-thurs starting at noon. Please see our homepage for directions.

We have plants for purchase

We grow plants year round in our green house! We have a variety of house plants and florals available for purchase inhouse. Large quanities are availble for preorder and delivery via inquiry.

We host events

We do weddings, garden parties, and custom events! Book us for your next event. We provide free event consultations!
