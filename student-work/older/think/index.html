Do you know why you do what you do?

Straight Thinking offers classes ranging from 1 week to 7 weeks that help its attendees clearly define their problems and come up with solutions.  Common logical fallacies are explored and people are interactively engaged in learning to reason for themselves.

In a world where things change rapidly, there are few constants.  But the ability to reason helps us to understand things as they happen and change.  The man who doesn't use his mind, has little advantage over the creature that doesn't have one.
